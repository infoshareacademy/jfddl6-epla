Add event

It's absolutely free. All you need is send us an email to the adress contactepla@gmail.com.
Tell us more detailed information (name, type, date, ticket price) about your event to make it easier to find for our users. Send us a photo and short description of event to locate it in our application.

Join event

Scroll through plenty of events and find those you'are intereseted in.
Sign up for the event you would like to participate in with special button added to every event and don't miss any of happening you are interested in.

Meet your friends

You are wondering where you can meet your friends and what events are they interested in?
Use our ePla to find your friends and check out the list of events they are interested in.

Locate event

New in a town or worried you'll miss a place of event you are interested in?
No worries! Scroll your screen to the event your are going to and use our map to localize your event. Connect it with google maps and let the application guide you to your destination place.

Filter events

Interested in sports, concerts, films, fantasy games or board games?
Filter events via categories and move only between those that interest you.

Today's events

Too busy to plan your free time?No problem!
Use today's function and search only the events of the day. Save your time and stay up to date with happenings taking place in your city.

Comment

Read, write and express yourself in comment section. Let the other users know your impressions, share your thoughts and exchange minds. Join an ePla community and set the pace of local events.
